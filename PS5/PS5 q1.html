The streamplot and nullclines demonstrate that three steady-state solutions exist: high delta 1 and low delta 2, low delta one and high delta 2, or equal delta 1 and delta 2. This is similar to the result seen in the limit of delta degradation much greater than notch degradation, although the delta concentrations do not reach their maximum values in the limit of this problem.
